01 Who we are

One team from investigation to foundation

Zenova Steel is a Singapore-based specialist contractor focused on underground construction and high-complexity demolition works.

With a strong foundation in geotechnical and heavy civil engineering, we manage the full lifecycle of substructure works — from investigation and obstruction removal through excavation support to foundation construction.

Our integrated approach delivers projects more efficiently by reducing coordination gaps between contractors. By consolidating engineering, manpower, machinery and materials under one team, clients get a seamless and controlled execution model.
